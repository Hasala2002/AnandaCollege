Ananda College

As an academic institution with a profound and prolonged history, Ananda College holds a unique position within the country due to its glory in many fields. Without a doubt it is one of the leading boys’ schools in the island. For the main part, Ananda College is known as the pinnacle of Buddhist education on the island. The glory of Ananda College has been echoing throughout the globe since 1886 with its alumni achieving great feats of success while more than 8000 students are studying to amplify the legacy of their predecessors. Ananda College provides education to students from grade 1 to 13, with a variety of streams for Advanced Level examination. These streams include biology, physical science, commerce, arts and engineering technology. Furthermore, with a campus of 10 acres, Ananda College offers its students many co-curricular and extra-curricular activities to suit themselves for the modern world with both brains and brawn.
